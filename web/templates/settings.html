How to Get a Google App Password

To authorize the security system to send alerts through your Gmail account:

  1. Go to your Google Account Settings > Security.
  2. Ensure 2-Step Verification is enabled.
  3. Under "2-Step Verification", scroll to the bottom and click on App passwords.
  4. Select app as Other (Custom name), name it (e.g. Smart Door System), and click Generate.
  5. Copy the 16-character password displayed on the screen and paste it into the Google App Password field above.
